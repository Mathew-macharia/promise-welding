About Us

Founded by master welder Haron Kamau with over 15 years of experience, Promise Welding has built a reputation for excellence across Nakuru County and beyond. Our commitment to precision craftsmanship and attention to detail ensures every project exceeds expectations.

We take pride in delivering high-quality metalwork that combines functionality with aesthetically pleasing designs. Whether you're looking for security gates, decorative window frames, or custom metallic solutions, we stand behind every piece we create.

What sets us apart is our reliability and ability to deliver projects nationwide. No matter where you are in Kenya, we can bring our expertise to you, ensuring your vision becomes reality without compromise.
